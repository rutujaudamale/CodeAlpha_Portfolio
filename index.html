About

Hi, I'm Rutuja — a third-year Diploma in Computer Engineering student based in Dombivli, building two things in parallel: a data analyst portfolio, and a small web development practice for local businesses.

On the data side, I work through the full pipeline — cleaning messy datasets, exploring them in Python and pandas, and packaging findings into Power BI dashboards that are actually readable by someone who isn't a data person.

On the web side, I design and ship sites for small businesses in Dombivli, handling everything from layout to free-tier deployment. I like projects that end with something real: a dashboard someone opens, or a site someone's business actually runs on.
